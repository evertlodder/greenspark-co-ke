About Greenspark (K) Ltd

13/07/2016

Greenspark is an engineering company specialised in solar applications for the commercial sector, large scale farming and industry. Over the years, Greenspark has gained extensive experience in EPC projects: engineering; supply and installation of grid connected solar installations; operations and maintenance service contracts; energy management installations.

Professional background

Greenspark BV was established in Netherlands in 2009 and is operational in Kenya since 2010. Greenspark (K) LTD has been established in 2014. Greenspark is accredited with the highest certification Service Partner Plus from Fronius, allowing us to perform maintenance and repairs under warranty. We have a long term supply agreement with JASolar, number two worldwide supplier of high quality cells and solar modules.
